Banan - the name of the edible fruits of cultivated species of the genus Banana (Musa); Musa acuminata and Musa × paradisiaca, and a number of others are usually understood as such. Also, bananas can be called fruits of Ensete ventricosum (strictly speaking, being a representative of another kind of family.
From a botanical point of view, a banana is a berry, mnogosemennoy and pachyderm. Cultivated forms often lack seeds that are not needed for vegetative propagation. They have dimensions up to 15 cm in length and 3-4 cm in diameter. Stems can consist of 300 fruits and have a mass of up to 50-60 kg
Bananas are one of the oldest food crops, and for tropical countries the most important food plant and the main export item. Ripe bananas are widely consumed throughout the world, they are used in the preparation of a large number of dishes.
